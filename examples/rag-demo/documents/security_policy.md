# Security Policy

## How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the emailed link. The reset link expires after 30 minutes for security.

## Do you support two-factor authentication?
Yes. Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available on all plans and can be enabled from your account security settings. We support authenticator apps and SMS codes.

## How is my data encrypted?
Customer data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher, and encrypted at rest using AES-256. Encryption keys are managed and rotated by our infrastructure provider.
