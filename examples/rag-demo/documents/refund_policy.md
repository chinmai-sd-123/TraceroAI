# Refund Policy

## How long does a refund take?
Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after the request is approved. The funds appear on your statement depending on your bank's processing time.

## How are refunds issued?
Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. We do not issue refunds as account credit or to a different card.

## Are annual plans refundable?
Annual plans can be refunded on a prorated basis within the first 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, annual plans are non-refundable.
