# Product FAQ

## Can I change my workspace region?
Customers cannot change a workspace region after the workspace is created. To move to a different region, you must contact support to request a migration; admins cannot do this themselves.

## What plans are available?
We offer three plans: Free, Pro, and Enterprise. The Free plan supports up to 3 members. The Pro plan supports up to 50 members and adds priority support. Enterprise adds SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated account manager.

## What is the maximum file upload size?
The maximum file upload size is 100 megabytes per file on the Free and Pro plans. Enterprise customers can request a higher limit by contacting support.
