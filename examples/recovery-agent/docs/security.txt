Security

Encryption
----------
Customer data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher, and at rest using
AES-256. Encryption keys are managed in a dedicated key-management service and rotated
on a fixed schedule. Backups are encrypted with the same standard as primary storage.

Authentication
--------------
To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the link
emailed to the address on file; reset links expire after 30 minutes. Accounts may
enable multi-factor authentication using a TOTP authenticator app or a hardware security
key. Enterprise accounts can enforce SSO, in which case local passwords are disabled and
all sign-in flows through the identity provider.

Compliance
----------
We are SOC 2 Type II certified, with the report available under NDA on request. We
support data-processing agreements (DPAs) for customers subject to GDPR, and we honor
data-subject access and deletion requests within 30 days. A current list of
sub-processors is published on the trust page.

Incident Response
-----------------
Suspected security issues can be reported to security@example.com. We acknowledge
reports within one business day. Confirmed incidents affecting customer data are
disclosed to affected customers in line with contractual and regulatory timelines.
