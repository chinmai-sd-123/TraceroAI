# Refund Policy

## Standard Refunds

Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after the request is approved. The
approval review itself typically completes within 1 business day of submission, so most
customers see the full refund land in their account within a week and a half at most.

Refunds are always issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. We
cannot redirect a refund to a different card, bank account, or wallet, because this is
a fraud-prevention requirement enforced by our payment processor. If the original card
has expired, the issuing bank will normally route the refund to its replacement; if the
account is fully closed, contact support and we will arrange a manual bank transfer.

## Subscriptions

Subscriptions can be refunded within 14 days of renewal. After the 14-day window the
current billing period is non-refundable, but you may cancel to prevent the next
renewal. Annual plans are refunded on a prorated basis if cancelled within the first 30
days; after 30 days, annual plans follow the same 14-day-from-renewal rule applied to
the most recent renewal date.

## Partial Refunds and Credits

For usage-based charges (such as overage on API requests), we issue partial refunds
only when the overage resulted from a documented service incident on our side. In all
other cases, usage charges are final. As an alternative to a cash refund, customers may
request account credit, which is applied to future invoices and never expires.

## How to Request

Open a refund request from Billing → History → the relevant invoice → "Request refund".
You may also email billing@example.com with the invoice number. Refund status is
visible in the same Billing → History view and moves through: requested → under review
→ approved → processed.
